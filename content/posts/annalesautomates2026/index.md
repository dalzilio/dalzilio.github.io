This Gitlab repository contains a set of exercises (in French language :fr:)
covering finite state automata, regular expressions, and some concepts of
context-free grammars (mostly regular grammar and LL(1) parser).

These exercises have been used to evaluate second-year students in the computer
science track at ENSEEIHT for the Automata and Language Theory course. In
particular, the repository includes a
[subdirectory](https://gitlab.laas.fr/dalzilio/annales-automates-et-theorie-des-langages/-/tree/main/YEAR?ref_type=heads)
containing past exam subjects, ranging from 2021 to 2025. 

These exercises are free to use and designed to help other professors draft
their own exam questions. This is why I will not provide any solutions. However,
these exercises can still be useful to students.
